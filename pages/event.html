    About us

    Precious Island works with private and public sectors to help youth initiatives towards Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through collaborations. We held the Global Youth Conference on SDGs in Indonesia every December as our commitment to promote various initiatives by youths in Indonesia and the world. We also worked with schools, universities, and youth organizations through our One Person One Tree programme and series of free webinars to help students creating innovative solutions that can be presented during the yearly conferences.
