    About Us

    We provide platforms for youth to take global actions on three different scales: Indiviual scale, Instiutional scale, Global scale.



    On Indiviual scale: Commitment to plant a million trees by indonesian youth as a commitment of young generations willingness to contribute towards Sustainable Goals



    On Institutional Scale: Create sustainable projects in schools, supported by headmaster and bring as many students to join the movement as possible, making a big impact on the institutional level





    On Global Scale: share progress, knowledge, and oportunities with others through PI Network and create new initiatives together through our annual Global Youth Conference for SDGs.



    About us

    Precious Island works with private and public sectors to help youth initiatives towards Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through collaborations. We held the Global Youth Conference on SDGs in Indonesia every December as our commitment to promote various initiatives by youths in Indonesia and the world. We also worked with schools, universities, and youth organizations through our One Person One Tree programme and series of free webinars to help students creating innovative solutions that can be presented during the yearly conferences.
